## üìù NOTES & BEST PRACTICES

1. **Content Repurposing:** Every Anchor piece should generate 10-15 derivative pieces
2. **Engagement:** Respond to comments within 24 hours on all platforms
3. **Accessibility:** All video content should have captions; PDFs should be screen-reader friendly
4. **Compliance:** All medical claims must be substantiated and reviewed
5. **Patient Privacy:** Obtain written consent for all patient stories and testimonials
6. **Evergreen Content:** Create timeless pieces that can be updated and reshared
7. **Seasonal Timing:** Align with awareness months (e.g., Crohn's & Colitis Awareness Week)
