# EPAVANCE Website Redesign Plan
## Complete Site Replication & Enhancement

---

## Executive Summary

This plan outlines the complete redesign of all internal pages at www.epavance.co.uk, applying the new pharmaceutical-grade aesthetic established in the homepage redesign. The goal is to create a cohesive, premium, and medically-credible web presence that positions EPAVANCE as a trusted pharmaceutical-grade supplement brand.

---

## Design Principles

### 1. **Pharmaceutical Credibility**
- Clean, clinical layouts with ample white space
- Medical-grade imagery (microscopy, molecular structures, clinical settings)
- Evidence-based content presentation
- Professional typography and color palette

### 2. **Visual Excellence**
- Teal (#006D77) and Mint (#83C5BE) color scheme
- Smooth animations and micro-interactions
- High-quality custom imagery (no stock photos)
- Glassmorphism and modern UI elements

### 3. **User Experience**
- Clear information hierarchy
- Easy navigation between related content
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast loading times

## Content Strategy

### **Tone of Voice**
- **Professional but accessible**: Medical credibility without jargon
- **Evidence-based**: Reference clinical studies and data
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledge patient challenges with IBD/gut health
- **Authoritative**: Position as experts in Omega-3 and gut health

### **SEO Considerations**
- Unique, keyword-rich page titles
- Meta descriptions for all pages
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Alt text for all images
- Internal linking strategy
- Schema markup for products and articles

### **Accessibility**
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Proper color contrast
- Keyboard navigation
- Screen reader compatibility
- Alt text for all images
- ARIA labels where needed
